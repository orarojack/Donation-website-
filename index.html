About Us

Clip Kenya (Candle Light Program) is a community-Based Organization , dedicated to making a significant and lasting impact in the lives of persons with disabilities within Kenya. At the heart of our work lies the philosophy of touching lives with compassion. We believe in creating sustainable change through a holistic approach, focusing on education, healthcare, environmental sustainability, and economic empowerment.
